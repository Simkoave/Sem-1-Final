The Inheritance Games

By: Jennifer Lynn Barnes

Summary:

A broke highschooler inherits billions of dollars from a random famous old man, but to get it, she has to stay with his grandchildren for a year.

Review:

Okay, I know this sounds really cheesey. That is also what I thought (ignore how I just finished book 5, all the novellas, and pre-ordered the 6th book). However, once you get over the start of the book, it gets really good! I really want a tv series for it. When the author releases books, she always posts cool puzzles that you have to solve to see parts of it early.

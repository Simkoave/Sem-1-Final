Adult Recommendations:

Butcher & Blackbird

By: Brynne Weaver

Summary:

Two serial killers who kill murderers fall in love.

Review:

So... I thought this was a differnt book. I only read it bc I thought it was a different book. I'm a bit dumb. The concept was good, but it sounds like it was written by a middle schooler. People are saying that the author is working with the director from The Lego Movie to turn this into a movie.

★★★☆☆


The Dead Romantics

By: Ashley Poston

Summary:

A girl falls in love with the ghost of her boss.

Review:

I was partner reading this with my mom. It was okay, but when I read this, I didn't read romances so I didn't like it.

★★★☆☆


Check & Mate

By: Ali Hazelwood

Summary:

A girl who hasn't played chess in years beats the #1 chess player in the world.

Review:

THE VIBES AAAAAAAAA! My friends constantly make fun of me that the only reason I can somewhat play chess is bc of this book (and Queens Gambit but I still haven't finished it yet). It was a bit depressing and I felt so sad for the characters. A bit predictable though.

★★★★☆


Young Adult Recommendations:


If He Had Been with Me

By: Laura Nowlin

Summary:

Follows a girl through her teenage years while she daydreams wha it owuld be like if she dated her neighbor.

Review:

IM NOT WEIRD MY FRIEND MADE ME READ THIS. I was wondering why so many people liked it because it was slighly boring, and then the ending happened. My mouth literally fell open.

★★★★☆


I Hope This Doesn't Find You

By: Ann Liang

Summary:

The top student's email drafts are leaked, which she vented in them, and are sent to the whole school, containing over 40 emails to her rival.

Review:

I LIVE LAUGH LOVE THIS BOOK OMGOMGOMGOMGOMGOMGOMG! This was so good and the protagonist was written so well aaaaaaaa!

★★★★★


The Cruel Prince

By: Holly Black

Summary:

A mortal girl starts her plan to take over the fae world but somehow falls in love with the fae prince that bullies her.

Review:

My friends also made me read this... they said it would get better... it did not get better. Look, the fantasy part and the world building and the politics were GREAT! However, that was some of the worst written romance I have ever read. Not to be self-absorbed but I could write better romance than that On the plus side, I did get an A+ on a hate essay I wrote about it..

★★★☆☆


XOXO

By: Axie Oh

Summary

A girl and a K-pop idol fall in love.

Review:

I was once again forced against my will. This was lowkey mid, but it was giving the power of friendship.

★★★☆☆


A Girl Who Fell Beneath the Sea

By: Axie Oh

Summary:

A girl sacrifices herself to the sea god and her fate is accidentally connected to his servant.

Review:

My friend made me read this (the same friend who made me read XOXO). This book has good world building and for some reason reminded me of Bubble Guppies.

★★★★☆

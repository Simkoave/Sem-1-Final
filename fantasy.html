Fourth Wing

By: Rebecca Yarros

Summary

A scribe is sent to go to the deadly training center for flying dragons.

Review:

So, these chapters are so long omgggggg. But, it does get so much better that I finished most of the book in one day!

★★★★☆

Young Adult Recommendations


War Hour

By: Lauren Losgic

Summary

People have to willingly earn their magic powers, but one kingdom starts forcing them.

Review:

This was so good! It had a completely different plot than I was expecting. Still predictable though.

★★★★☆


Powerless

By: Lauren Roberts

Summary

People without powers are banned, and the ones with really strong powers face trials. A girl without magic powers is put into the deadly trials.

Review:

OMG THIS WAS SO GOOD! I regret not reading it sooner. The dialogue was amazingly written.

★★★★★


Assistant to the Villain

By: Hannah Nicole Maeher

Summary

A broke girl stumbles upon her villages most hated villain and he offers her a job.

Review:

This was cheesey, but still really good! It was so funny! Kind of predictable, but the ending actually wasn't exactly what I expected.

★★★★☆
